Welcome to the BMEG Optimization Tool

Welcome to the Building Material Environmental Guide (BMEG) Optimization Tool. The BMEG Optimization Tool identifies optimal materials for constructing buildings. You are able to enter information about your region to find suitable materials as well as specify materials available in your region.

To use the app:

Fill out the survey about your region to specify any building requirements. Then select materials which are available to you for building.
